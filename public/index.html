Our Model

Our model car contains four sensors, which can obtain data from all four directions and display on our control panel. If the car meets obstacles within a particular distance (depends on the visual range of the sensors), the images of the current situation (proposly 1-2 images) taken by a servo controlled camera attached on top of the vehicle will be sent back to the server to alert users. You can deliver commands to the car, deciding the direction of the car via the web page buttons.

How to use

You can control the car by simply pressing the interactive buttons on the control page. For a more immersive experience, you can also use the precision mode, “WASD” control. You will be able to view the live capture by clicking the “Warning IMG” button on the main control page.

W: forward full throttle
S: reverse gear
A: turn left
D: turn right
